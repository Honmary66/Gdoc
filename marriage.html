Marriage Registration Procedures

Welcome to the official guide for marriage registration. Follow these steps for a smooth and efficient process:

  1. Visit the local government office responsible for marriage registration.
  2. Complete the birth marriage certificate form.
  3. Gather identification documents such as passports, birth certificates, and proof of residence for both partners. Include any divorce decrees or death certificates if applicable.
  4. Head to the local marriage registrar office or city/town hall where marriages are registered.
  5. Submit your marriage application.
  6. Collect the official marriage certificate from the register office.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, don't hesitate to contact us.
